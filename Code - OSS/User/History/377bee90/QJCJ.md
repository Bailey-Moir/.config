The [[Orthogonal Vectors|orthogonal]] projection of $\mathbf{\underline{b}}$ is given by:
$$ \text{orth}_\mathbf{\underline{a}} \, \mathbf{\underline{b}} = \mathbf{\underline{b}} - \text{proj}_\mathbf{\underline{a}} \, \mathbf{\underline{b}} $$
